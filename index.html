Mission

Delight in Every Connection

With the advancement of technology, there are many new different channels of communication and social media today. This has given way to a generation with limitless access to information, and connections across the world.

As much as this is wonderful, new changes bring in new challenges. Sometimes these connections that are made can be negative. Whether it is cyberbullying or fake news, adish believes that as a society, we must always continue to challenge to eliminate such negative use of connections.

This is why adish’s mission is to provide a “Delight in Every Connection”. At any time, generation, or place, we will work to maintain the delight in a connected world.

Service

Customer Success

We believe providing customer support for apps and web services, shouldn’t just end there. In order to create user loyalty, we believe that it is customer success to be pursued. Other than customer support, we also provide customer activation, localization, not only multilingually, but multiculturally.

Social Media Management

From consulting, strategizing and operating we take care of official social media accounts, such as Twitter, Facebook and Instagram. We manage social media as a tool of marketing and engagement, and to nurture the online community.

Internet Monitoring

Internet monitoring is a way we make sure that there is no negative viral content. We can create guidelines, and monitor comments and sites 24/7, to create a safe online environment.

How We Work

Maximize the potential of the individual and the team

Maximize the potential of the individual and the team

To ensure that everyone can maximize their potential, our office is based on the concepts of freedom and flexibility.

Certain desks are fixed for teams, whilst we also have free desk areas, where people can find their perfect spot.

The office isn’t just for tapping away on the keyboard, but a place where we connect with each other face-to-face. It is our base, where we come together to work towards our goals.

The Little Extra Mile

The Little Extra Mile

adish employees have their own responsibility to plan out their work. As a company, we want to provide the best workplace for our employees to work at their full potential, the way they want. That’s why we want to go the little extra mile with some of the following benefits.

Exempt Work

Your work style can be designed to fit your lifeplan. We believe in the win-win when your personal life goals align with the company’s.

Extra-curricular Support

Extra-curricular activities such as learning languages or applying for qualifications, are all encouraged through financial support.

QCP

Every quarter, our employees evaluate their performance together with their superiors. Setting goals and wages are all within the individuals’ power.

Book Purchase Support

The company will financially support any purchase of books or resources for education purposes.

Reviews

Whether it is your direct superior or a manager of a different division, you are free to set meetings called “reviews”. Here, you can discuss anything.

Stock Options

The performance of the company relies on its employees. We thus provide stock options to our employees.

Rest Space

Our rest space consists of couches, a TV, games and magazines. It is a little getaway to have some alone time or mingle and have some fun.

Drink Bar

Not everyone drinks coffee. That’s why we also have drinks like black tea, Japanese milk tea, green tea, and hot cocoa, to meet the other needs.

Company Events

Twice a year, people fly in from our branch offices for company events. The winter we focus on our mission and business, and in summer, we go out to have team building recreation.

Sharing Service Support

As a company that supports the sharing economy, we provide some extra financial support for the use of sharing services.

Recreational Discounts

We provide discounts on hotels, gym membership, theme park entries and more throughout the year.

Restaurant Discounts

Our employees are eligible to discounts at a number of restaurants.
